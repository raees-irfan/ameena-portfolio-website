Our Services

Play and Behaviour Therapy

We use play-based and behavior-focused techniques to help children express emotions, build social skills, and manage challenges like tantrums, attention issues, or anxiety. Through engaging, child-led sessions and practical strategies, we support their emotional growth in a safe and supportive environment.

Child & Adolescent Counselling

Our counselling services support children and teens facing emotional, social, or academic challenges. Through age-appropriate conversations, creative expression, and therapeutic guidance, we help them manage anxiety, low self-esteem, peer pressure, family changes, and more. We create a safe space where young minds feel heard, supported, and empowered to grow.

Disability and Rehabilitation Services

We provide personalized support for children with physical, developmental, and intellectual disabilities to help them build independence and improve daily functioning. Our rehabilitation services focus on enhancing motor skills, mobility, sensory integration, and adaptive behavior through targeted therapy plans. By collaborating closely with families, educators, and medical professionals, we ensure a holistic approach that empowers every child to thrive in their environment and reach their fullest potential.

Developmental and Psychological Assessments

We offer comprehensive developmental and psychological assessments to understand each child's cognitive, emotional, and behavioral profile. These evaluations help identify learning difficulties, developmental delays, attention challenges, or emotional concerns. Our goal is to provide accurate insights and clear recommendations to guide personalized therapy, school support, and family care.
